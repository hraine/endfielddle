About the Game

ENDLE is a daily guessing game based on Arknights: Endfield. Try to guess the operator or character by their attributes or emojis!

How to Play

Operators Mode: Guess the daily operator by their class, weapon, element, and rarity. You have 8 attempts.

Emoji Game: Guess the character by emojis. Start with 1 emoji, and get 1 more emoji with each wrong guess (max 4 emojis).

Features

  • Daily challenges with seed-based character selection
  • Autocomplete with character portraits
  • Progressive hints system
  • Archive to track your progress
